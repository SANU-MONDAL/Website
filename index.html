Vision

To establish commercial relationship with companies or a new business start-up (that may be founded by the researcher) and catalyze the commercialization of technologies for global public benefit. Provide resources and training to Tufts researchers on matters relating to intellectual property protection, technology development, technology transfer, and entrepreneurship.

Mission

  • To assist the researchers during the start-up phase and protect their intellectual property and devise appropriate strategies for transferring the intellectual property from the university to industry .

  • To guide the researchers in administrative, financial and legal matters during the entire project life cycle throughout the process of translating a business idea into a real company 

  • To protect the innovations and manage technology development projects to enhance the value of these innovations in the marketplace and the reputation of Tufts as a leading research institution 
    •

    Objectives of Technology Transfer

  • Collaborating with industry: To build a relationship with surrounding industry and involve those for large scale production by technology transfer .

  • Managing intellectual property:To protect their intellectual property and devise appropriate strategies for transferring the intellectual property from the university to industry;

  • Creating spin-off companies: To assist researchers during the start-up phase and guides them through the process of translating a business idea into a real company.

  • Promoting entrepreneurship and innovation: To stimulate networking initiatives and technology clustering to small scale industries.
